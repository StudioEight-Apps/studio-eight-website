Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Delete Account. You'll be asked to confirm three times before your account and all data are permanently deleted.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Open Settings ‚Üí Manage Membership, or go to your iPhone Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí Whisper and cancel from there.
Is my journal data private?
Yes! Your journal entries are encrypted and stored securely. We never share or sell your personal data.
Can I export my journal entries?
You can view all your past entries in the History tab. For data export requests, contact support@whisper-app.com.
How does the AI work?
Whisper uses advanced AI to understand your reflections and generate personalized mantras that help guide your inner dialogue.
I found a bug. How do I report it?
Email us at support@whisper-app.com with details about the issue and we'll fix it ASAP!
