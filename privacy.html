Privacy Policy – Mantra: Smart Journal

Effective date: August 29, 2025

1. Data We Collect

We do not sell data. Apple, Google, and Firebase may process limited info strictly to provide authentication, payments, and app infrastructure.

We use standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS). You may request deletion by contacting support@studioeight.app.
